Our story

Beauty with intention.

Maison Aurelia began with one belief: luxury should be measured by how long something matters.

A quieter idea of luxury

We bring together independent ateliers and established houses united by their respect for material, proportion, and craft. Every piece is chosen slowly, with attention to its origin and its life beyond a single season.

Our standards

We favor natural fibers, traceable production, and makers who preserve specialist skills. We ask whether a garment feels exceptional, whether it can be repaired, and whether it will remain relevant years from now.

Made personal

Our client team offers thoughtful guidance on fit, fabric, care, and wardrobe building. The relationship continues long after a piece leaves our store.
